Soccer Heads European Edition

By Mousebreaker

In this game you try to score goals with your feet. Take your position according to the ball that bounced your opponent. Dribbling with the ball for flying volley or jumping header. To score more goals you should be good rebounder. Focus on rebounds, goal and opponent's position in the pitch.

Spanish Word Attack

By Linguascope

The object of the game is not to let the words stack up to the top of the pile! To make a word disappear before it reaches the bottom, type its translation into the box. But beware.. the higher the score, the quicker the words fall!
